About Gudauri Ski Resort

Gudauri Ski Resort was founded by passionate skiers and mountaineers who wanted to create a world-class skiing destination in Georgia.

Our mission is to provide an unforgettable skiing experience while promoting sustainable tourism and preserving the natural beauty of the Caucasus Mountains.

We are dedicated to offering high-quality services and facilities to all our guests.
