Our Story

Gudauri Ski Resort was founded by a group of passionate skiers and outdoor enthusiasts who envisioned creating a world-class winter sports destination in the heart of the Caucasus Mountains. Since our establishment, we have been dedicated to providing exceptional experiences for all our visitors, from beginners to seasoned athletes.

Our mission is to offer top-quality services and facilities that enhance the natural beauty and excitement of our location. We value safety, sustainability, and the joy of outdoor adventure. At Gudauri Ski Resort, every guest is treated like family, and we strive to create memories that last a lifetime.
